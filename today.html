Treaty 3 Today

What Treaty 3 is like today

Population

Today about 25,000 Indigenous people living on Treaty 3 which is 55,000 sq. miles. This population inculeds 26 First Nations from Northwest Ontario and two from southeasetern Manitoba.

Services

As of April 2003, Treaty 3 has it own Police force. Treaty Three Police Srtvice (TTPS) is funded by both provincial and federal governments.
